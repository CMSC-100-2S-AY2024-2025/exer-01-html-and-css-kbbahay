About me

My name is Kent Benedick A. Bahay, and I was born on July 25, 2004, in Taytay, Rizal. Our family now resides in Pasig City, Manila. I have three siblings, and I am the second child. My parents, Rosalie Adriano Bahay and Benedicto T. Bahay, have always been my greatest support system.

      I completed my elementary education at De Castro Elementary School in Pasig and proudly graduated from Pasig Science High School with pride and honor. After Senior High School, I applied for the DOST Scholarship Program to support my college education, as I would be studying far from my hometown. Fortunately, fate favored me, and passed both the UPCA and the DOST Scholarship Program, which brought me to where I am today.

      I recently graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of the Philippines, an achievement that has equipped and honed me with the knowledge and skills. My college journey was both challenging and rewarding, pushing me to grow not just academically but also personally. Throughout my academic journey, I developed technical expertise, analytical thinking, and a deep appreciation for innovation in technology. Additionally, my experiences inside and outside the classroom have strengthened my adaptability, collaboration, and critical thinking skills.
